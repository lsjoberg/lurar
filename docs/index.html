System-wide audio EQ
for macOS.

A menu bar parametric EQ that taps every app's audio via Core Audio, runs it through a 10-band biquad chain, and plays the result on your DAC. No virtual driver, no kernel extension, no subscription.

Download for macOS

Requires macOS 14.2 or later · Apple Silicon & Intel

Per-headphone presets

Ships with a Flat preset. Browse the AutoEq catalog (Oratory1990 measurements) and add curves for your headphones in seconds.

A/B compare

Sighted or blind switching between two presets. Find out which curve you actually prefer, not the one that looks right on paper.

Crossfeed & loudness

Built-in crossfeed for binaural-friendly imaging. ISO 226:2003 loudness contour compensates for low-volume listening.

No virtual driver

Uses Apple's Core Audio Process Tap API directly (macOS 14.2+). Nothing to install, nothing to uninstall, nothing to break on the next macOS update.
